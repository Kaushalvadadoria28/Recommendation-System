About | My Book Recommender

This website has been designed for the deployment of a Machine Learning model. The purpose of this ML model is to provide personalized book recommendations based on an individual's reading history. The "Home" section utilizes a popularity-based recommendation system, while the "Recommend" section employs a collaborative filtering recommendation system. In the "Recommend" section, you can enter the name of a book, and the model will subsequently suggest similar books to you.
